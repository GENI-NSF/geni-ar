Reset password to a GPO IdP account

Fill out the form below to request a password reset link for an existing account used to access the GENI Portal and Clearinghouse. Follow the link you are emailed and fill out the form to pick your new password.

You can then use this account to log in to the Portal by identifying your institution as "GENI Project Office" and supplying the username and password you chose here. If you do not already have an account and would like to request one, use the account request page instead.
